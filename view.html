What is your vield of view from cruising altitude?

Please bare in mind when using our route maps, that you cannot see objects directly under the plane. Depending on angle and distance to the window, your field of view starts at approximately two to five miles.

You will be able to distinguish man-made objects up to 15 miles, after that they are simply too small for the human eye to recognize.

That does not mean that you can’t see further than 15miles. Mountain ranges and coast lines can be identified well beyond that. At an altitude of 30’000 feet you the horizon is 211 miles away.
